What is ShadowNet?

ShadowNet is a small social-style website inspired by Twitter, built to demonstrate basic web design using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

How the site works:

The site consists of three main pages: Home, Posts, and Contact.

  • Home

    A simple landing page introducing the site.

  • Posts

    A section where users can view short posts, similar to tweets.

  • Contact

    A page that allows visitors to send email to the site owner.

Helpful info

On the Posts page, you can click any username to open a profile modal with details like name, email, and address.

On the Contact page, the Send button only unlocks after you confirm you're ready to send.
